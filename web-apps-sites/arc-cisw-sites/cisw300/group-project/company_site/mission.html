Mission

Our Mission is to make "good" drivers, "safer" drivers! We offer four different courses that guarantee you become a safer driver.

Over time the human and vehicle damage in our society has grown to accept as a part of daily life, and the cost of being on our roads can be effectively reduced not eliminated.

Response Time

What we experience in our daily lives is the result of vehicle operators finding themselves in unfamiliar life and death situations with 1/4 of a second to respond and face the consequences and not knowing what to do, and what to do next.

(Similar to dropping a piece of fine china and not knowing how to catch it.)

Self Confidence

No training system can ever make anyone perfect, BUT our program WILL improve any vehicle operator's skills and confidence far beyond 10%. We Guarantee it !!

Our training is not designed to replace any other driver training system only to supplement them.

Our training is not designed to and will not teach you how to drive or pass a drivers examination or test.

Our training is specially developed to teach and improve your VEHICLE HANDLING SKILLS in unfamiliar (scary) situations, as well as routine operation of any vehicle in any conditions.

Our system will and has been proven to work in any vehicle (front - rear wheel drive, standard - automatic transmission, sub compact - trucks/bus)
